Accreditation Visits

1-Zero Visit for those Institutes, that launch new computing program.
2- Interim Visit Interim Visit for those batches, which are yet to be accredited. The Interim Visit will be conducted during the Second/Third year of the program.
3- Accreditation Visit Accreditation visit will be conducted in the final year of those batches, which are going to be graduated.

REVISED ACCREDITATION FEE STRUCTURE OF CSAS

The CSAS Accreditation Fee has been increased from Rs. 100,000/- to Rs. 150,000/- per computing program w.e.f July 1, 2013. A BANK DRAFT OF RS. 150,000/- PER COMPUTING PROGRAM PAYABLE TO CSAS MUST BE SUBMITTED WITH ANY APPLICATION RECEIVED AFTER JULY 01, 2019

Welcome to CSAS's new website

Required Faculty and Students Information

CSAS added Students and Faculty information forms named "Institution-Faculty Information" and "Institution-Students Information" along with 16 Application Forms. It is to Inform all Institutes to provide Students and Faculty Information by filling these forms along with Accreditation Application. CSAS will not process the accreditation application till the Institutes provide the faculty and students information as CSAS Required.

Computer Science Accreditation System

Federal Urdu University of Science And Technology has setup an accreditation authority, Computer Science Accreditation System (CSAS). CSAS will look after the matter regarding the accreditation of departments of the institute and , faculties and disciplines by giving them appropriate ratings and define the organization's objectives, functions and duties to be performed. It will periodically evaluate, scrutinize and monitor the standards followed in different Universities, Degree Awarding Institutions and their affiliated colleges offering computing degree programs.
